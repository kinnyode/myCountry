Grand Canyon, USA.

The Grand Canyon, located in Arizona, USA, has a geological history spanning millions of years. Carved by the Colorado River, the canyon's formation began around 5 to 6 million years ago. It has served as a home to various Native American tribes and was first explored by European settlers in the mid-19th century. Today, the Grand Canyon National Park attracts millions of visitors who come to admire its breathtaking beauty and unique geology.
