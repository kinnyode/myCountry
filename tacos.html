Tacos.

Tacos have a history rooted in ancient Mesoamerican cultures, with evidence of early tortilla-making dating back over 2,000 years. The Aztecs are credited with inventing the first tacos, using tortillas as a vessel for various fillings. Tacos evolved over time, with Spanish influences introducing new ingredients such as beef and cheese. They gained worldwide popularity, becoming a beloved Mexican street food and a culinary staple enjoyed in numerous variations.
