Stonehenge, UK.

Stonehenge, located in Wiltshire, England, is a prehistoric monument believed to have been constructed around 2500 BCE. Its purpose and construction methods remain a subject of speculation. Composed of massive standing stones, it is thought to have served as a burial site and a place of religious or ceremonial significance. Stonehenge is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and continues to intrigue and captivate visitors with its enigmatic history.
