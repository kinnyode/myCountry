Pyramids of Giza, Egypt .

The Pyramids of Giza, located near Cairo, Egypt, were built during the Old Kingdom period of ancient Egyptian history. The construction of the pyramids, including the Great Pyramid of Khufu, began around 2580 BCE and lasted for several decades. These monumental structures served as tombs for pharaohs and were built with impressive precision and engineering. Today, they stand as a testament to the incredible architectural and cultural achievements of ancient Egypt, attracting visitors from around the world.
