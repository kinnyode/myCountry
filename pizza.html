Pizza.

Pizza originated in Naples, Italy, in the late 18th century. Initially a simple flatbread topped with tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil (representing the Italian flag), it gained popularity among the lower classes. In the late 19th century, pizza migrated to the United States with Italian immigrants and evolved into various regional styles, such as New York and Chicago-style. Today, pizza is enjoyed worldwide, with countless variations and flavors.
