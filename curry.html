Curry.

Curry has a rich history that dates back over 4,000 years. Originating in the Indian subcontinent, curry was initially a mix of spices used for flavoring dishes. It spread to other parts of Asia and the world through trade and colonization, evolving into diverse regional styles. Today, curry is a globally beloved dish, prepared in various ways, with each culture adding its unique twist to the flavorful combination of spices.
