French.

French, a Romance language, originated from the Vulgar Latin spoken in the region of Gaul during the Roman Empire. It began to develop as a distinct language around the 9th century CE. With the rise of the Kingdom of France, French became the language of the court and administration. It further spread during the era of colonial expansion. Today, French is spoken by millions worldwide and serves as an official language in numerous countries and international organizations.
