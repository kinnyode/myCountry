Sushi.

Sushi originated in Japan around the 8th century. It started as a method of preserving fish by fermenting it with rice. Over time, the fermentation process evolved into using vinegar to preserve fish with rice. Sushi gained popularity as a delicious and convenient snack, and in the 19th century, sushi chefs began to experiment with different ingredients and techniques, leading to the diverse and artful sushi we enjoy today.
