Welcome to Europe.

Rome, founded in the 8th century BCE, grew from a small city-state to become the center of one of the world's greatest empires. The Roman Republic, established in 509 BCE, expanded through conquest and political reforms. Julius Caesar's assassination in 44 BCE led to the rise of the Roman Empire, which lasted until 476 CE when it fell to barbarian invasions. Rome's legacy includes its legal system, engineering marvels like aqueducts and roads, and cultural influences that endure to this day.
