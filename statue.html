The Statue Of Liberty, NYC.

The Statue of Liberty, gifted by France to the United States, was dedicated on October 28, 1886. Designed by Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi, it stands on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. The statue represents freedom and democracy and has become an enduring symbol of the United States and its ideals. Standing at 305 feet (93 meters), it continues to attract millions of visitors annually.
