Taj Mahal, India.

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century as a mausoleum for his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Constructed from 1632 to 1653, it is renowned for its stunning white marble architecture and intricate decorative details. The Taj Mahal is considered a masterpiece of Mughal architecture and is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It stands as a symbol of love and is visited by millions of tourists each year.
