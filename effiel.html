Eiffel Tower, Paris.

The Eiffel Tower, designed by Gustave Eiffel, was completed in 1889 for the Exposition Universelle (World's Fair) held in Paris. Standing at 1,063 feet (324 meters) tall, it was initially met with mixed reactions but has since become an iconic symbol of Paris and France. Today, it is one of the most visited monuments in the world and offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.
