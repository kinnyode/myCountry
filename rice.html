Rice & Noodles.

Rice has been cultivated for over 10,000 years, originating in China and spreading to other parts of Asia. It became a staple food in many cultures, including India and Southeast Asia. Noodles, on the other hand, have a long history in China, dating back to at least 4,000 years ago. They spread to other parts of Asia and the world, evolving into various forms and styles. Today, both rice and noodles are integral parts of diverse cuisines globally.
